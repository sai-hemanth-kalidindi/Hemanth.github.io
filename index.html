NYC Traffic Fatalities Analysis

Jan 2024 – May 2024

  • Analyzed 2M+ crash records from Vision Zero datasets.
  • Used regression & statistical tests to explore fatality patterns.
  • Created Tableau heatmaps for Midtown & Brooklyn risk zones.

Title & Objective

Investigate spatial, behavioral, and temporal factors influencing traffic fatalities in NYC neighborhoods.

Data Source

NYC Open Data - Vision Zero Crash Dataset (2012–2024).

Key Insights

Brooklyn and Manhattan show consistently higher fatality rates. Alcohol involvement and nighttime crashes had higher death risk. Fatality spikes occurred in 2013 and 2021.

Reflections

This project challenged my ability to clean large datasets and select the right statistical tools. Creating spatial visualizations was especially rewarding for policy impact.
