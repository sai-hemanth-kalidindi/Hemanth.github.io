NYC Traffic Fatalities Analysis

Tools: Python, SQL, Tableau

Objective: Investigate spatial, behavioral, and temporal factors influencing traffic fatalities in NYC neighborhoods.

  • Analyzed over 2 million crash records from Vision Zero datasets.
  • Applied OLS regression, Chi-Square tests, and T-tests to evaluate borough-level fatality risks.
  • Mapped crash hotspots using heatmaps—Midtown and Brooklyn showed higher fatality clusters.
  • Designed visual dashboards to aid policymakers in safety-focused urban planning.

Formula 1 Crash Trends (1950–2020)

Tools: Python, Regression Analysis

Objective: Analyze how technological and safety interventions like HALO, run-off zones, and regulation changes affected crash frequency in F1 races.

  • Parsed 70 years of crash, retirement, and qualifying data from F1 archives.
  • Time-series regressions revealed steep drop in fatal crashes post-2010 safety reforms.
  • Highlighted high-risk circuits like Spa and Nürburgring based on historical crash density.
  • Correlated team investment, vehicle power, and safety innovations with retirement types.
